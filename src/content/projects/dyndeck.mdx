## About DynDeck

DynDeck is a presentation tool that rethinks how we create and deliver presentations. Built with local-first principles in mind, it ensures your content is always available and under your control.

### Features

- **Local-First Design**: Your presentations live on your machine first
- **Dynamic Content**: Embed live code, real-time data, and interactive elements
- **Offline Reliability**: Work anywhere, anytime, without depending on the cloud
