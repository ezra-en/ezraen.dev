## About Ladder

Ladder is an experiment in making browser tab management more intuitive through spatial organization and context awareness.

### The Problem

Browser tabs have become our primary interface with information, but managing them remains a challenge. Ladder approaches this problem from a new angle.

### The Solution

By treating tabs as spatial objects that can be organized contextually, Ladder provides a more natural way to manage your browsing workspace...
